# Oni Theory — Social Media Product Posters

Generated social-media posters for the **Oni Theory** catalog (onitheory.com).
All posters are **4:5 Instagram portrait** (1080×1350 equivalent).

Two formats were produced:

- **Higgsfield "DTC Ads" (photorealistic marketing ads)** — the product photo turned into a
  cinematic, on-theme campaign visual. Viewable in your Higgsfield library, or via the CDN links below.
- **Canva (editable branded posts)** — on-brand Instagram posts (product + headline + price + `SHOP NOW`),
  built with your Canva brand kit. Open the edit links to tweak copy/layout and export.

> Note: the Higgsfield CDN links are the rendered output. If a link ever expires, the same image
> is saved in your Higgsfield account. Canva edit links are permanent designs in your account.

## How these were made

- **Higgsfield** model `marketing_studio_image` (DTC Ads), 4:5, product photo used as image reference,
  with a per-product themed prompt (Neo-Tokyo neon, shrine dusk, stormy lightning, etc.).
- **Canva** `instagram_post` (1080×1350) generated with the Oni Theory brand kit and the product photo,
  then saved as an editable design.
